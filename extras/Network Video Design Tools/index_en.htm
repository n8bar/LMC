About Axis
The driving force in network video Committed to open standards
Axis is the world leader in network video. We were first to bring the benefits of IP to video surveillance and remote monitoring, introducing the world’s first network camera in 1996. With nearly three decades of network know-how, full commitment to open industry standards and strong partnerships, Axis delivers solutions that best answer users’ current and future needs – including a complete range of high-quality network cameras, video encoders, video management software and accessories.
Built on VAPIX®, Axis' open application programming interface, our solutions ensure easy integration and scalability by using open technology standards, and standard networks and IT equipment. As a founding member of ONVIF, Axis works to ensure compatibility and interoperability between systems.

With the world’s largest installed base of network cameras, Axis offers unrivalled expertise. Together, we create video surveillance solutions that expand the user’s potential.
